# Mara — Carpenter

## Age
Early 40s

## Appearance
Mara has broad shoulders and strong arms from years of labor. Her hair is usually tied back in a messy knot, often dusted with sawdust. She moves with purpose and confidence.

## Clothing
- Work apron
- Utility belt
- Heavy gloves
- Sturdy boots

## Visual Read
Someone who fixes problems instead of talking about them.

## Role in Town
Repairs structures and builds what little the town still needs.

## Backstory
Mara stayed when others left because she believes things don’t fix themselves. She has repaired the same bridge more than once and knows it will break again someday—but that doesn’t stop her from fixing it now.

## Narrative Purpose
Represents rebuilding and persistence.

## Gameplay Purpose
- Repairs the broken bridge
- Handles town upgrades
- Enables house improvements

## Personality & Tone
Direct, practical, quietly optimistic.
