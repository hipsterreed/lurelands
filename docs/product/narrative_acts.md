# Narrative Acts

The narrative of LureLands is structured into acts that align with player progression, mechanical depth, and environmental change. Each act introduces new questions while slowly resolving earlier ones.

Each narrative act aligns with biome progression defined in WORLD_MAP_AND_BIOMES.md.


---

## Act 1 — Legacy at Risk

**Themes:** Belonging, responsibility, quiet urgency  
**Player Goal:** Protect the family home, relearn fishing, reconnect with the town

- Player wakes in their family home, a place they have lived in for years
- A notice arrives explaining the outstanding lien on the property
- The town is hosting its annual fishing contest, kept alive mostly by tradition
- The player receives their first fishing pole at the docks
- Fishing is reintroduced as a way to earn money and pay down the lien
- NPCs reference the player’s family history and how the town used to feel
- Mentions of a legendary fisherman surface during the tournament
- NPC dialogue reflects longing rather than expectation

**Narrative Purpose:**  
Anchor the player emotionally. This act establishes personal stakes, the core gameplay loop, and the first hints of mystery.

---

## Act 2 — Restoration & Memory

**Themes:** Nostalgia, rebuilding, unanswered questions  
**Player Goal:** Restore areas, help villagers, uncover fragments of the past

- New fishing locations unlock as the player restores old paths and docks
- NPC quests reveal personal memories tied to fishing and the town’s decline
- The player discovers old tools, journal fragments, and forgotten fishing spots
- Conflicting accounts of the legendary fisherman begin to emerge
- The environment subtly changes as waters begin to respond

**Narrative Purpose:**  
Deepen emotional investment and complicate the mystery. The town begins to feel alive again, even as the truth remains unclear.

---

## Act 3 — Truth & Continuation

**Themes:** Legacy, balance, quiet resolution  
**Player Goal:** Understand the fisherman’s intent and decide what the future looks like

- The player uncovers the fisherman’s true relationship with the waters
- Lost knowledge of fishing techniques and locations is restored
- Legendary fish and hidden areas are revealed
- The town regains confidence, not perfection
- The lien is resolved, but the story continues

**Narrative Purpose:**  
Resolve the mystery without turning the player into a savior. The ending emphasizes continuation, not replacement.
