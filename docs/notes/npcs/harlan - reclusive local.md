# Harlan — Reclusive Local

## Age
Early 50s

## Appearance
Harlan is gaunt, with a wild beard and sharp, restless eyes. His movements are erratic—sometimes still for long periods, sometimes sudden and intense.

## Clothing
- Long, worn coat
- Hood or scarf
- Mud-stained boots
- Carries strange, improvised fishing tools

## Visual Read
Someone people warn you about.

## Role in Town
Lives on the outskirts and avoids the town center.

## Backstory
Harlan spent years fishing places others avoided. He claims the water speaks back if you listen long enough. When fishing declined, he didn’t leave—he went deeper into isolation.

Some believe he knows things no one else does.

## Narrative Purpose
- Mystery escalator
- Unsettling voice in the world
- Foreshadows runes and strange biomes

## Gameplay Purpose
- Hints about runes
- Optional and unsettling quests
- Early foreshadowing of the mushroom biome

## Personality & Tone
Erratic, intense, unsettling honesty.
