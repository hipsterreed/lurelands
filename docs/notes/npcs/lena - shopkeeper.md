# Lena — Shopkeeper

## Age
Late 30s

## Appearance
Lena is sharp-eyed and alert, always aware of what’s happening around her. Her hair is neatly tied back, and her workspace is meticulously organized.

## Clothing
- Simple tunic
- Ledger pouch
- Clean boots
- Gloves when handling fish

## Visual Read
Someone who notices patterns others miss.

## Role in Town
Buys fish, sells supplies, and manages trade.

## Backstory
Lena stayed because leaving didn’t make sense financially—or emotionally. She has watched inventory shrink and customers disappear, but she keeps the shop running efficiently.

## Narrative Purpose
Grounds the story in economic reality and visible progress.

## Gameplay Purpose
- Buy and sell fish
- Accept lien payments
- Provide early gear upgrades

## Personality & Tone
Practical, observant, dry humor.
