# Caleb — Young Optimist

## Age
Early 20s

## Appearance
Caleb is energetic and expressive, often moving faster than necessary. His clothes are practical but worn, and he often carries a small pack or notebook.

## Clothing
- Light jacket
- Simple boots
- Backpack
- Small trinkets or notes

## Visual Read
Someone eager to see what’s possible.

## Role in Town
One of the few younger residents who never left.

## Backstory
Caleb grew up hearing stories of how things used to be but never saw them firsthand. He believes the town can change because he’s already seen small improvements since the player started fishing again.

## Narrative Purpose
Represents the future and mirrors player impact.

## Gameplay Purpose
- Side quests
- Exploration nudges
- Emotional reinforcement

## Personality & Tone
Hopeful, curious, encouraging.
