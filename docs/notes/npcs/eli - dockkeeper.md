## Age
Late 50s

## Appearance
Eli is tall and lean, with weathered skin and hands permanently marked by salt and rope. His hair is gray and kept short, usually hidden beneath a faded cap. His posture is relaxed but tired, like someone shaped by years near the water.

## Clothing
- Faded work jacket
- Rolled-up sleeves
- Heavy boots
- Old fishing gloves tucked into his belt

## Visual Read
A man who belongs to the docks. Even standing still, he feels anchored to the shoreline.

## Role in Town
Maintains the docks and oversees fishing activity.

## Backstory
Eli worked alongside the legendary fisherman in his younger years. He never claimed to understand the waters the way the fisherman did, but he learned what patience and respect looked like. When the fisherman disappeared, Eli stayed, maintaining the docks long after they stopped being used.

## Narrative Purpose
- Primary connection to fishing history
- First NPC to reference the legendary fisherman
- Emotional anchor of nostalgia

## Gameplay Purpose
- Introduces fishing mechanics
- Oversees the fishing contest
- Central NPC in the bridge repair questline

## Personality & Tone
Quiet, observant, thoughtful. Speaks slowly and chooses his words carefully.
