# Thomas — Innkeeper

## Age
Mid 60s

## Appearance
Thomas is rounder than he once was, with a thick mustache and expressive eyebrows. His movements are slower now, but his eyes light up when he tells stories.

## Clothing
- Buttoned vest
- Rolled sleeves
- Worn apron
- Polished shoes from habit

## Visual Read
A storyteller who remembers better days vividly.

## Role in Town
Runs the town’s inn and tavern, the last remaining social hub.

## Backstory
The inn was once full every night. Thomas remembers laughter, arguments, celebrations, and exhaustion. As the fishing dried up, the crowds faded—but he kept the doors open.

## Narrative Purpose
- Lore distributor
- Social memory of the town

## Gameplay Purpose
- Quest hub
- Rumors and story hints

## Personality & Tone
Warm, talkative, nostalgic, welcoming.
