# Ellie — Town Clerk

## Age
Early 60s

## Appearance
Ellie is neatly dressed and carefully composed. Her hair is always pulled back, and she carries herself with quiet authority.

## Clothing
- Formal jacket
- Glasses
- Ledger always nearby
- Sensible shoes

## Visual Read
Structure and order personified.

## Role in Town
Manages property records, notices, and official matters.

## Backstory
Ellie has watched the town shrink through paperwork—closed homes, unpaid debts, abandoned names. She enforces the lien not out of cruelty, but because structure is the only thing holding the town together.

She wants the player to succeed, even if she never says it outright.

## Narrative Purpose
Represents consequence without villainy.

## Gameplay Purpose
- Delivers the lien notice
- Tracks debt progress
- Triggers story milestones

## Personality & Tone
Formal, fair, restrained compassion.
